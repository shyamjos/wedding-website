Event Details

Wedding Mass

Mass starts promptly at 11:00 AM. Out of respect for the sacraments, we kindly request all attendees arrive by 10:40 AM. As marriage is a Sacrament, this is the main event and the most important part where the marriage happens! The Wedding Mass will be done in the Tridentine Rite (Traditional Latin Mass) at St. Stephen Church. This Mass consists of two parts: the marriage service, and the Nuptial Mass. The exchange of vows and blessing from the priest occurs prior to the Mass during the marriage service, and the Nuptial Mass follows where the bride and groom participate in their first Eucharist as husband and wife.

Reception

The wedding reception is a place for members of both families and friends of the bride and groom to socialize amongst themselves and get to know each other. A formal dinner and wedding cake will be provided. This is a dry event, and alcohol will not be provided. Why no alcohol?

RSVP Deadline

Please RSVP by July 15, 2024, to ensure your spot at the celebration.

Dress Code

Formal attire is requested. Attire should be follow dress for the Traditional Latin Mass. Ladies should wear floor length or past-knee length dresses and gentlemen should wear suits and ties. We ask that everyone – men and women – come to Mass dressed modestly. We kindly request attendees to refrain from wearing the color Purple.
